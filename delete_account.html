Account Deletion Request

We are sorry to see you go. If you wish to delete your LoveSpace account and all associated data, please follow the steps below.

Steps to Delete the Account

  1. Open the LoveSpace application on your mobile device.
  2. Navigate to the Profile tab.
  3. Tap on the Settings icon (gear icon) in the top right corner.
  4. Select Manage Account.
  5. Tap on Delete Account.
  6. Confirm your choice by reading the warning and tapping Confirm Deletion.

What Data Gets Deleted

Deleting your account is permanent. Once completed, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Profile Information: Your name, email address, profile picture, and bio.
  • Relationship Data: Anniversary dates, shared widgets, and connection status with your partner.
  • User Content: All shared notes, messages, images, and other media uploaded to the service.
  • App Activity: Game scores, usage history, and preferences.
Note: This action cannot be undone. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

Data Retention

Upon confirmation of deletion:

  • Your account data is immediately removed from our active databases.
  • For technical reasons, copies of your information may remain in our backup systems for up to 30 days. This data is not accessible for normal use and is automatically overwritten in due course.
  • We may retain strictly necessary logs (non-personally identifiable) for security and analytical purposes as required by law.
